(Team)TPG Antidoxx Tool (how and why?)









What is this tool and how do I use it?

When playing TeamTPG or when you just want to share a CSV of your images with someone, it is very easy to doxx yourself. That’s why I made this tool. It strips sensitive locations out of the CSV.

How it works:

  • Upload a CSV with image coordinates and optionally any number of additional columns.
  • Define one or two sets of blacklist coordinates – places you want to hide.
  • Each blacklist point has an inner radius (100% removal zone) and an outer radius (fades to 0% chance of removal).
  • Optionally, you can click on the header of a column in the table to toggle whether that column should be kept or removed.
  • Everything runs entirely in your browser — your data never leaves your computer. In fact, if you don’t need to load additional map tiles, you can turn off your internet connection after loading the page.

Map colors:

  • Red ring: Inner radius — all points within this circle are removed.
  • Orange ring: Outer radius — points between the red circle and this circle are removed with decreasing probability the farther they are from the center.
  • Green dots: Kept points.
  • Blue dots: Removed points.
